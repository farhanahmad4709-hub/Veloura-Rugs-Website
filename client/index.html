A Legacy Woven in Time

At Veloura Rugs, we believe a rug is more than a floor covering — it is a living artwork, a bridge between cultures, and an heirloom passed across generations. Since 1985, our family has sourced the finest hand-knotted rugs directly from master weavers in Persia, Turkey, Afghanistan, and Morocco.

Every piece in our collection is authenticated and carries a certificate of origin. We work hand-in-hand with artisan communities, ensuring fair compensation and the preservation of ancient weaving traditions.
